Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use PelvicFit Men, we collect:

  • Quiz responses: Your answers to our health assessment quiz, used to personalize your training plan.
  • Email address: Provided by you to receive your personalized plan and program communications.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers.
  • Usage data: Browser type, device, pages visited, and referral source (via analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your personalized pelvic floor training plan
  • To process your payment via Stripe
  • To send you your plan, program updates, and relevant health content via email
  • To improve our quiz, content, and user experience
  • To run advertising campaigns and measure their effectiveness

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Meta (Facebook): For advertising optimization via the Meta Pixel
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and funnel optimization
  • Email service provider: For email delivery and marketing communications

4. Email Communications

By providing your email, you consent to receiving:

  • Your personalized training plan PDF
  • Program-related updates and tips
  • Promotional offers for related products

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

5. Data Retention

We retain your quiz responses and email for as long as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing support@pelvicfitmen.com.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and tracking pixels (Meta Pixel, Google Analytics) to measure advertising effectiveness and improve our services. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@pelvicfitmen.com for any data requests.
